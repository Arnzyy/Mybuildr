# Scaling Assumptions

## Current Stage
Marketing Website (Launch Phase)

## Expected Scale

| Stage | Monthly Visitors | Timeline |
|-------|------------------|----------|
| Early | 100-500 | Now (launch) |
| Growth | 1,000-5,000 | Month 3 |
| Scale | 5,000-20,000+ | Year 1 |

## Current Architecture Supports
- ✅ Up to 100k visitors/month on Vercel (more than enough)
- ✅ Image optimization for performance
- ✅ Fast initial load with no database queries
- ✅ Mobile responsive design
- ✅ SEO-friendly

## Non-Goals (For Now)

NOT building until sales demands:
- ❌ Complex analytics dashboard
- ❌ A/B testing framework
- ❌ Multi-language support
- ❌ Custom booking system (Calendly handles it)
- ❌ Email automation
- ❌ CRM integration

## When To Revisit

Revisit this when:
- Getting 50+ qualified leads/week and want to track conversion better
- Need to test pricing page variants
- Considering international expansion

## Upgrade Path

| Bottleneck | Solution | Cost |
|------------|----------|------|
| Analytics | Vercel Analytics + Plausible | $10/mo |
| Hosting | Vercel Pro | $20/mo |
| Email followup | Sendgrid or Mailgun | $0-20/mo |
