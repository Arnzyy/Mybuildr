# APP SPECIFICATION: Construction Website Platform Marketing Site

## Overview

**App Name**: [TBD - BuilderHub/SiteFlow/ConstructKit]
**One-liner**: Marketing site for construction website + automated social media platform
**Primary Goal**: Convert warm leads from Check a Trade outreach into demo calls/emails
**Secondary Goal**: Rank for "construction websites UK" and related searches

**Messaging**: "Professional websites + automated social media posting for construction companies. £99-199/month."

---

## Target Users

### Primary Persona: "Local Builder"
- Solo traders to small teams (1-10 people)
- Currently online (website) or not online at all
- Exhausted from manual social media or not doing it at all
- Expects to pay £500+ for a website
- Shocked to see £99 tier
- Will trust peer testimonials

### Secondary Persona: "Small Construction Company"
- 10-30 people
- Trying to do marketing but no bandwidth
- Looking for quick wins
- Price-sensitive but value-focused

### Where They Live
- Check a Trade (primary outreach channel)
- Google search for "construction websites" (secondary)
- Word of mouth (tertiary)

---

## Core User Journey

1. **Discovery**: Receive email on Check a Trade or Google search
2. **Landing**: Click link to marketing site
3. **Validation**: Scroll through and see:
   - Real construction site example
   - Real Instagram feed with automated posts
   - Testimonial from peer
   - Pricing (surprised it's affordable)
4. **Action**: Email for demo
5. **Conversion**: Reply with options, book call or start build

## Page Structure (Single-Page Scroll)

### Section 1: HERO
- Headline: "Professional websites + automated social media. £99-199/month."
- Subheading: "Stop losing customers because you're not online."
- CTA: "Email us for a demo"
- Visual: Modern SaaS hero, subtle construction imagery

### Section 2: THE PROBLEM
- Copy: "Your competitor posts 5x/week. You're not online at all (or posting once a month)."
- Visual: Before/after comparison (competitor's Instagram feed vs builder's neglected)
- Stat: "Time wasted not having a website"

### Section 3: HOW IT WORKS (3-Step)
- Step 1: "We build it" (week turnaround, templates)
- Step 2: "You upload images" (admin panel, drag-drop, idiot-proof)
- Step 3: "We post automatically" (Instagram + Google Business, you set frequency)
- Each step has icon + brief copy

### Section 4: INTERACTIVE DEMO
- Switchable tabs/cards showing:
  - **TAB 1**: Real construction website (first client's site, fully clickable)
  - **TAB 2**: Admin panel screenshot (showing image upload, posting schedule)
  - **TAB 3**: Instagram feed (showing automated posts from their account)
- Copy above: "See it in action"
- Goal: Show entire workflow in 30 seconds

### Section 5: PRICING
- Three columns: £99 | £149 | £199
- £99 = Website + Hosting
- £149 = + Admin panel (upload own images)
- £199 = + Automated Instagram/Facebook/Google (full package)
- Lead with £99 but position £199 as recommended
- All have email CTA

### Section 6: TESTIMONIAL (Social Proof)
- Real quote from first client (the "chuffed" guy)
- Client name + business name + photo (if available)
- Simple 1-2 sentence quote about transformation

### Section 7: FAQ
Question 1: "How long does it actually take?"
Answer: "Your site is live and posting within a week. Sometimes faster."

Question 2: "What if I want to change stuff?"
Answer: "Email us. We handle updates quickly on a ticket system."

Question 3: "Do I have to upload images?"
Answer: "Yes, you upload project photos to the admin panel. It takes minutes per week."

Question 4: "Can I cancel anytime?"
Answer: "Yes. Month-to-month, no contract."

Question 5: "What if I already have a website?"
Answer: "We can rebuild it or migrate it. Email to discuss your situation."

### Section 8: ABOUT US (Brief)
- 2-3 sentences max
- "We build websites for construction companies because we understand the business"
- Mention: We run businesses, we get it
- Not rambling, not personal stories

### Section 9: FOOTER CTA
- Final push: "Ready to get your business online? Email us."
- Footer contact info
